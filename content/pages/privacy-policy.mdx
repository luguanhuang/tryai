## Introduction

Welcome to **Trys.AI** ("we", "us", or "our"), an AI SaaS code template and platform experience that helps users quickly build AI SaaS products and try multiple AI features in one place.  
This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you visit **https://trys.ai** or use related services.

By accessing or using Trys.AI, you acknowledge that you have read this Privacy Policy.

## Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

1. **Account Information**  
   Information such as your name, email address, and login credentials when you create or use an account.

2. **Usage and Log Data**  
   Information about how you interact with our site and services, including page visits, feature usage, timestamps, and diagnostic logs.

3. **Device and Technical Data**  
   Browser type, operating system, IP address, device identifiers, and similar technical data.

4. **AI Input and Output Data**  
   Prompts, files, and generated outputs you submit or receive when using AI-related features.

5. **Payment Information**  
   Billing and transaction-related data. Payment card processing is generally handled by third-party payment providers, not stored in full by us.

6. **Cookies and Similar Technologies**  
   Data collected through cookies or similar tools for login state, preferences, analytics, and service performance.

We use collected information to:

- Provide, maintain, and improve Trys.AI services
- Authenticate users and secure accounts
- Process payments and subscriptions
